Vijñana 2025

Vijñana 2025 is a celebration of innovation, creativity and cutting-edge technology. As our college's premier tech fest, we aim to bring together bright minds to explore, challenge and redefine the boundaries of technology. From interactive workshops to thrilling competitions. Vijñana 2025 is the ultimate platform for students to showcase their skills, ignite ideas and shape the future. Join us as we push the frontiers of tech and inspire a generation!

About Our College

Situated in the heart of Thalassery, Kerala, Kodiyeri Balakrishnan Smaraka Government College is a prestigious institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development. The college offers a wide array of programs that foster innovation, creativity and leadership. With a strong emphasis on quality education and a vision for empowering students, the college stands as a beacon of learning in the region. It continues to nurture young minds, preparing them to excel in diverse fields and contribute to society's progress.
