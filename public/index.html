Vijnana 2025

Vijnana 2025 is a celebration of innovation, creativity and cutting-edge technology. About Our College

Situated in the heart of Thalassery, Kerala, Kodiyeri Balakrishnan Memorial Government College is a prestigious institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development. The college offers a wide array of programs that foster innovation, creativity and leadership. With a strong emphasis on quality education and a vision for empowering students, the college stands as a beacon of learning in the region. It continues to nurture young minds, preparing them to excel in diverse fields and contribute to society's progress.
